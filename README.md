## Features

### Core Gameplay
- Place your house on a 230×15 tile grid shared with all players
- Earn coins through farms, trivia, neighborhood bonuses, and collecting items
- Buy items, send messages, and interact with other players' houses
- Cashout coins to real money once you reach 1,000

### Buildings
- **Farm** (500₪): Place next to your house to generate an egg every hour worth 20 coins
- **Power Plant** (row 90): New landmark - buy an energy subscription to boost farm income
- **Azrieli Mall**: Shop for items (flowers, bikes, PCs, and more)
- **Knesset**: Buy and sell items from your inventory
- **Trivia**: Answer questions to earn 10–20 coins
- **Leaderboard**: See the top 10 richest players
- **Synagogue**: Read the weekly Torah portion (parasha)
- **Yad Sara**: Make a donation
- **Candle Building**: Light a memorial candle (40₪)
- **Eilat**: Visit the southern harbor

### Upgrade Features

#### 1. Farm Upgrade
Click on your own farm to open the farm panel. Upgrade your farm to produce more coins per egg:
- **Level 1** (default): 20 coins/egg/hour
- **Level 2** (600₪): 40 coins/egg/hour
- **Level 3** (1,200₪): 80 coins/egg/hour

#### 2. Power Plant Subscription
Visit the Power Plant building (row 90 on the board) to buy a 7-day energy subscription for 400₪.
While subscribed, every farm egg you collect gives you **+20 bonus coins** on top of your base production.
Subscriptions stack with farm upgrades and VIP status.

#### 3. VIP Upgrade
Click **"שדרג ל-VIP"** in the sidebar (1,500₪, one-time purchase).
VIP benefits:
- **+20 coins** on every farm egg collection (stacks with power subscription)
- **Double neighborhood bonus**: 100 coins/day instead of 50
- Gold 👑 VIP badge shown next to your name

**Maximum earning rate** (Farm L3 + Power + VIP): 120 coins/egg + 100 coins/day neighborhood bonus
